Municipality of Ibaan

Ibaan is a municipality located in the province of Batangas in the Philippines. It is situated in the Calabarzon region on the island of Luzon. Ibaan is known for its agricultural products, including rice, sugarcane, and various fruits. The town is also rich in cultural heritage, and it celebrates various festivals and events throughout the year, showcasing its traditions and local customs. The local economy is driven by agriculture, trade, and some industries. Ibaan is a relatively small and peaceful town, providing a rural and serene environment for its residents.
